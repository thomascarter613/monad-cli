# Evolution Constraints

## Purpose

Evolution constraints define the rules that prevent unsafe or ungoverned change.

Constraints preserve institutional stability.

---

## Evolution Constraint Doctrine

Evolution constraints must ensure:

```text
change is safe
change is governed
change is invariant-preserving
change is traceable
change is explainable
```

---

## Evolution Constraints

- No architectural change without ADR  
- No domain change without glossary update  
- No rule change without policy update  
- No mutation change without apply report  
- No release change without evidence  

---

## Evolution Constraint Invariants

```text
no ungoverned change
no unsafe change
no unexplained change
```
