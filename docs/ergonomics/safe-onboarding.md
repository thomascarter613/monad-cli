# Safe Onboarding

## Purpose

Safe onboarding ensures that contributors enter Monad safely, with governed understanding of its architecture, governance, and invariants.

Onboarding is a safety process.

---

## Safe Onboarding Doctrine

Safe onboarding must ensure:

```text
contributors understand governance
contributors understand invariants
contributors understand evidence
contributors understand mutation safety
```

---

## Safe Onboarding Stages

### Stage 1 — Workspace Model

### Stage 2 — Lifecycle Graph

### Stage 3 — Plan-Backed Mutation

### Stage 4 — ADR Corpus

### Stage 5 — Work Packets

### Stage 6 — Release Evidence

### Stage 7 — Governance & Policy

---

## Safe Onboarding Invariants

```text
no contributor without governance understanding
no contributor without evidence understanding
no contributor without mutation safety understanding
```
