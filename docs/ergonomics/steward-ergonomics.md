# Steward Ergonomics

## Purpose

Steward ergonomics ensures that stewards can operate Monad safely, correctly, and sustainably.

Stewardship is institutional.

---

## Steward Ergonomics Doctrine

Steward ergonomics must ensure:

```text
stewards understand governance
stewards understand invariants
stewards understand evidence
stewards understand mutation safety
```

---

## Steward Responsibilities

- Maintain invariants  
- Maintain governance  
- Maintain evidence  
- Maintain provenance  
- Maintain roadmap  

---

## Steward Ergonomics Invariants

```text
stewards must be supported
stewards must be safe
stewards must be governed
```
